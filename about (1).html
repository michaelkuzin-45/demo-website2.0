Our Story

Built on
Trust

Volt & Flow started in 2013 when founder Ray Deluca got tired of watching Rockville homeowners get overcharged by national chains that treated them like ticket numbers.

Ray had 8 years as a licensed plumber and brought in his friend Tony Marsh, a master electrician, to build something different — a local company where the owners show up on jobs and stand behind every repair.

Twelve years later, we've fixed over 4,000 homes across Montgomery County. We still answer our own phones. We still show up on time. We still guarantee every job.

We're your neighbors. Our families live in Rockville, Bethesda, and Potomac. This community is where our kids go to school. That matters to how we do this job.
