Do you give free estimates?+
Yes — all estimates are completely free. We come out, assess the job, and give you a flat-rate quote before any work starts. You are never obligated to proceed.
How quickly can you come out?+
Same-day available if you book before noon. Most non-emergency jobs scheduled within 1–2 days. Emergency calls get a 45-minute average response, 24/7.
Do you charge hourly or flat rate?+
Flat rate, always. We quote the full price before we start. No hourly surprises — what we quote is what you pay, even if the job takes longer.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes. Maryland Master Plumber #4821 and Journeyman Electrician #7304. We carry $2M liability insurance and worker's comp on every job.
What if something goes wrong after the job?+
Every job comes with a 1-year workmanship guarantee. If anything we fixed stops working within a year, we come back and fix it free.
Do you do both plumbing AND electrical?+
Yes — both under one roof. One call for jobs that involve both trades, like bathroom remodels, kitchen updates, or whole-home inspections.
